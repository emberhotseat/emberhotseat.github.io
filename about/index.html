DeVaris Brown, your host for the Ember Hot Seat, is the API Guy for Zendesk by day and part time Ember.js enthusiast. When he's not preaching the gospel of the Zendesk developer platform to the masses, you'll find him with his hands firmly planted on a camera or a set of turntables. His goal for doing this podcast is to break down the barriers people have in developing with Ember, and not make it sound like there's a substitute science teacher speaking. He's not as bad as the picture looks now is he?
