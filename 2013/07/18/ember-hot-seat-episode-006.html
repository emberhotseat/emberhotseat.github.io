Ember Hot Seat Episode 006: James Rosen

18 Jul 2013

Continuing our busy week on Ember Hot Seat, we have my fellow Zengineer, James A. Rosen! James gives insights as to how we're using Ember at Zendesk including the long road in upgrading to 1.0, internationalization, data persistance, testing, and a lot of other cool things we do. James also gives some tips for staying happy and his personal favorite recipe he learned while studying culinary arts in Italy. I call it the "Abuse of Power" episode, but man does it feel classy :-) I hope you all enjoy!

-DeVaris
