Ember Hot Seat Episode 004: Toran Billups

16 Jul 2013

Hey there Embereños! Sorry for the delay in updates. It was the holiday season, so that meant travel for everyone, including myself. I'm fortunate to have my midwest brethren, Toran Billups on the show this week. Toran dives into his journey into Ember from a Python developer's perspective, testing with Ember(shoutout to Erik Bryn), getting his team sold on Ember, and how important it is to give back to the community. As always, please leave comments and feedback in the post or on our Twitter handle @emberhotseat. Thanks again for tuning in!

-DeVaris
