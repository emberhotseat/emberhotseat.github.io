Ember Hot Seat Episode 008: Alex Matchneer

24 Jul 2013

Slowing down on the prolific rate of output a bit, we have this week's guest Alex Matchneer Alex talks about his inspiration about the Ember Router in RC6, emblem.js, and Embercasts. We also talk about his journey into the open source world and gives advice on how people can get started.

I want to apologize in advance for some of the sound glitches. Skype was not being very friendly that day. Thanks again for listening and I hope you enjoy.
