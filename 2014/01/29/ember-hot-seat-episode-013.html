Ember Hot Seat Episode 013: Erik Bryn

29 Jan 2014

Welcome back! Sorry for the hiatus, but I come bearing gifts. This week we have Ember Core Team member Erik Bryn in the Hot Seat. We talk about HTMLBars and his not so scientific but cool anyways showdown with React, upgrading Zendesk to Ember 1.X, Ember testing, the landscape of the Ember economy, and the hierarchy of his favorite bourbons. If you've seen Erik talk, you know his appreciation of fine whiskey and bourbon. Also if you haven't done it already, please grab your tickets for EmberConf. To say they have been selling like hot cakes would be an understatement. Until next time.
