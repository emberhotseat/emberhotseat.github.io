Ember Hot Seat Episode 014: Jeremy Mack

08 Apr 2014

This week in the Hot Seat, we have Jeremy Mack from SparkBox. We talk about his amazing Ember application fnd.io, his development toolset he built with Brunch called tapas with Ember, and his unique history in the Air Force. We also talk about how he got started with Ember and even have an exclusive sneak peek into some coming features for fnd.io. Hope you enjoy!
